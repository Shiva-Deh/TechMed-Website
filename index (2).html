About TechMed

Health concerns shouldn't be confusing.

TechMed is an accessible, AI-powered symptom-checking application that helps patients better understand their health concerns. It guides users to describe, analyze, and understand their symptoms end-to-end, producing clear insights they can discuss with a healthcare provider.

We're a growing team of builders who care about making health information understandable for everyone — no medical dictionary required.

TechMed is built for people who want:

  • Accessible health guidance — check symptoms easily, anytime, anywhere
  • Clear, understandable insights — less confusion, more actionable next steps
  • Better-informed conversations — arrive at appointments already understanding your concerns

FAQ

Questions, answered

What is TechMed?
TechMed is an accessible, AI-powered symptom-checking application that helps patients better understand their health concerns before they see a healthcare provider.
Is TechMed a replacement for a doctor?
No. TechMed helps you understand and organize your symptoms, but it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider.
Who can join the team?
Anyone! Developers, designers, writers, researchers, and people passionate about health accessibility are all welcome. Fill out the Get Involved form and we'll be in touch.
Do I need a medical or coding background?
Not at all. There are roles across development, design, content, outreach, and research — enthusiasm and reliability matter most.
How can my organization sponsor TechMed?
Email us at yutechmed@gmail.com with the subject "Sponsoring TechMed" and we'll share how you can support the project.
